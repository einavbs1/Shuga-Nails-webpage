Our Courses


Gel Nail Manicure Course for Beginners

Looking to venture out on your own with financial security and engage in a field you love?
This course is the perfect solution for you.
The course is suitable for individuals with no prior knowledge in the nail field,
and it will teach you from scratch all the knowledge you need to perform gel nails at the highest level!
You will receive a complete equipment list from us and professional guidance even after completing the course.
The course lasts for a month, with 2 sessions per week, each session lasting for 4 hours. Total of 32 hours of study.




Decoration Course

Ever had a client show you a picture of a decoration she wants,
and you had to politely decline because you didn't know how to do it? No more.
In the decoration course, I will teach you techniques for creating complex decorations easily and quickly!
You can charge extra for decorations and easily increase your income.
The course consists of one session lasting for 4 hours.




Building Course (nails forms)

Ever had a potential customer message you to book an appointment,
only to find out that you don't offer nail building services?
Or had a client with a broken nail and didn't know how to fix it?
This course is suitable for manicurists who work or have studied in the field
and want to add another treatment to their business.
In the course, we will learn building in gel in different textures and methods.
The course consists of 2 sessions, each lasting for 4 hours.
